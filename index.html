Sudoku Game

Sudoku is a puzzle that needs little introduction. The object is to put a digit from 1 to N in each cell of the grid so that every row, column, and bold region contains each digit once in 3*3 box, where N is the number of rows and columns in the puzzle(Usually N=9). Sudoku puzzles come in endless number combinations and range from very easy to extremely difficult taking anything from five minutes to several hours to solve. Sudoku puzzles also come in many variants, each variant looking differently and each variant offering a unique twist of brain challenging logic.
However, make one mistake and you’ll find yourself stuck later on as you get closer to the solution.......Lets Play the Game!!!.
